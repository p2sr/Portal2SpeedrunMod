You don't have to go THAT slowly!

It started as a simple hack for speedrunners, now aims to be the greatest toy box Portal 2 Speedrunning Community could ever receive. Portal 2 Speedrun Mod is a modification that alters the way you run through the default Portal 2 campaign. Not only it removes majority of cutscenes and downsides, making the game a lot more entertaining to speedrun, but also introduces several game-changing modes, giving a fresh experience into your favourite test chambers, allowing you to enjoy them in a new unique way.

Normal mode

The main and original mode of Speedrun Mod. It's also a base to all other modes.

  • Most of the game's downtime is removed or shortened (like elevators, doors etc.)
  • Most of cutscenes were removed or shortened (like Tube Ride, Core etc.)
  • A full list of every change can be found here.

Celeste 2

A bunch of berries were scattered around Aperture. Dash your way through test chambers to collect them all!

  • Celeste-like movement added on top of current one (dash, wave dash, wallclimb and many more).
  • Certain test chambers were modified in order to fit into the gameplay/teach mode's mechanics.
  • 125 unique collectable berries hidden all around the levels:
    • Red Berries - difficult to find or difficult to get... sometimes both, sometimes none.
    • Quantum Berries - they don't seem to like portals...
    • Golden Berries - no.
  • Special ending allowing you to easily identify and collect berries you missed.
It is recommended to know the source material (Celeste) before playing the mod.

fog% (beta)

Put your muscle memory into test. Can you run the game with limited sight?

  • Dense fog makes you unable to see distant areas.
  • If you don't move for 20 seconds, you can see through fog until you move again.
  • Some say this mode will never exit beta testing...

Installation guide

  1. Make sure you have original game (Portal 2, duh) installed on Steam.
  2. Download the newest release from the link available above.
  3. Unzip Portal 2 Speedrun Mod folder into sourcemods directory.
    (its default location is Steam/steamapps/sourcemods/).
  4. Restart Steam.
  5. A new game, called "Portal 2 Speedrun Mod" should appear in your library.
